Design Conferences in 2022

Design
February 23, 2022

In the ever-evolving design landscape, staying current with trends, techniques, and networking is crucial. Design conferences offer the perfect opportunity to achieve all three while gaining inspiration from industry leaders.

Top Design Conferences This Year

2022 brings an exciting lineup of both virtual and in-person design events. Here are some you shouldn't miss:

1. Adobe MAX

The creativity conference returns with both online and in-person options. Expect workshops on the latest Adobe products, inspirational talks from creative professionals, and networking opportunities with thousands of designers from around the world.

2. OFFF Barcelona

This festival of creativity, art, and digital design brings together some of the most innovative minds in the industry. The three-day event features workshops, talks, and performances that push the boundaries of what's possible in design.

3. UX London

Focusing on user experience design, this conference combines inspirational talks with hands-on workshops. It's perfect for designers looking to enhance their UX skills and connect with others in the field.
